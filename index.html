Theoretical Physics

The Genius Behind the Theory of Relativity, Einstein Revolutionized Our Understanding of Space, Time & Gravity.

Galileo Galilei

Astronomy & Physics

The Father of Modern Observational Astronomy, Galileo Championed Heliocentrism & Improved the Telescope.

Key Achievements:

  • Discovered Jupiter's four largest moons
  • Observed phases of Venus
  • Improved telescope design

Fun Fact: He was placed under house arrest for supporting the idea that Earth orbits the Sun!

Vikram Sarabhai

Space Research

The father of India's space program, Sarabhai established ISRO and pioneered space research in India.

Key Achievements:

  • Founded Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
  • Launched India's first satellite Aryabhata
  • Established space research institutions

Fun Fact: He was only 28 when he established the Physical Research Laboratory in Ahmedabad!

Homi J. Bhabha

Nuclear Physics

The father of India's nuclear program, Bhabha established the foundation for nuclear research and atomic energy in India.

Key Achievements:

  • Founded Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR)
  • Established India's nuclear program
  • Pioneered cosmic ray research

Fun Fact: He was also an accomplished artist and classical music enthusiast!
